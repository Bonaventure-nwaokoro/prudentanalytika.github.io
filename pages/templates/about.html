About Prudent Analytika:

Prudent Analytika is a data analytics software company that uses computer software tools and services to analyse streams of data from Business units, Schools and other Organizations where the science of data analysis can be applied, with the aim of automating routine tasks and extracting raw data that will be processed into useful information for a more efficient functioning of the organization . Our aim is to apply data science to our client organization, so as to improve productivity and make the organization more efficient.
